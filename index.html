About Star Wars

Star Wars is a legendary sci-fi franchise created by George Lucas. It began in 1977 with *A New Hope* and has since grown into one of the most successful film franchises in history.

  • James Earl Jones provided the voice for Darth Vader, but David Prowse wore the suit.
  • The phrase "I have a bad feeling about this" is said in every Star Wars film.
  • Yoda was almost played by a monkey wearing a mask.
